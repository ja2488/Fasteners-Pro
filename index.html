Q: Which units does Fasteners Pro use?

A: You can work in metric or imperial. Torque: N¬∑m or ft¬∑lbf. Force: N or lbf. Area: mm¬≤ or in¬≤. Stress: MPa or psi.

Q: My torque seems high/low. What should I check first?

  • Material & grade/spec (and that its properties match your source).
  • Nut factor (K) and lubrication/finish condition.
  • Thread class (fit) and effective engagement length.
  • Correct diameter, pitch/TPI, and tensile stress area.
  • Unit settings (no hidden unit mismatch).

üß™ Best Practices for Reliable Torque Results
